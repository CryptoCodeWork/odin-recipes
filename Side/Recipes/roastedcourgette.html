Ingredients

  • Two small courgettes
  • Salt
  • Dusted garlic
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  • === (1) ===

  • Peel the courgettes and remove the tips.

  • === (2) ===

  • Cut in thin and long slices.

  • === (3) ===

  • Drizzle with olive oil and pulverize with the salt and garlic.

  • === (4) ===

  • Take to the oven until the edges start to brown.

Done!

Ready to serve. Goes really well with the  beef skewers.
