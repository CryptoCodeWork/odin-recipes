Creamy mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 100g Mushrooms (sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 10 Spinach leaves
  • 1tbsp of philadelphia light cheese
  • 1tsp of milk

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil on a pan and add the mushrooms. Cook until they brown.
  2. Add the spinach and mix.
  3. On a bowl mix the cheese and milk and then add the mix into the pan. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Done!

Ready to serve. Goes served with thin cut steak or chiken breasts.
